About Me

Architecting solutions.
Empowering teams.

I'm a Software Architect and technology leader freelancing through JoyB Works, based in Agartala, Tripura, India. With a Bachelor of Technology in Computer Science & Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Silchar and spanning enterprise programmes, venture‑backed startups, and global consulting engagements — I've evolved from hands‑on engineer to strategic technology leader: the kind who can still read the diff and own the platform roadmap in the same afternoon.

I've driven technology transformation across Pharma, FinTech, InsurTech, HR‑Tech, and AI — not as a vendor dropping deliverables, but as a strategic partner asking the harder questions: Is this the right architectural bet for the next three years? What does this decision do to engineering velocity at scale? How do we build AI into the product, not just onto it? I champion Domain‑Driven Design, event‑driven architecture, and composable AI platforms because durable competitive advantage is built in the foundation, not the feature list.

Engineering excellence is organisational, not just technical. I build engineering cultures — developing individual contributors into tech leads, tech leads into architects, and establishing the governance, hiring bars, and AI‑forward practices that let teams compound their output year over year. My goal is the leadership table where technology strategy and business strategy converge — because the best technology decisions and the best business decisions are, ultimately, the same decision.
